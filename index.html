Our story

A history of firsts

AMD Global's story is a success story of physician-driven development — a Houston-based firm that has spent years turning clinical insight into completed, operating healthcare infrastructure.

What began as a physician-centric vision has grown into a full-service development and investment firm. Along the way, AMD pioneered new healthcare delivery formats — developing one of the first free-standing emergency centers, one of the first micro-hospitals, and Class A medical office space — while expanding into urgent care, imaging, wound care, and medical specialties. Every project since has carried the same DNA: facilities designed by people who practice medicine, built to the very highest ethical, moral, and qualitative standards.

About the CEO

The physician-founder behind AMD Global's development model.

Dharmesh Patel, MD

Chief Executive Officer

AMD Global's founder and CEO, Dharmesh Patel, MD, is an emergency room physician.

Prior to establishing AMD Global, Dr. Patel was Co-Founder of Neighbors Emergency Centers and served as Chief Operating Officer from 2009 to 2015. During his tenure, he helped steer and expand Neighbors Emergency Center from its infancy to one of the largest independently held systems of freestanding emergency rooms in the nation, with more than 30 locations across the United States. Dr. Patel later shifted his healthcare development prowess to AMD Global to deliver some of the nation's leading medical, billing, and real estate assets.

Through Dr. Patel's vision, AMD Global has expanded its list of projects to include Class-A medical office buildings, ambulatory surgery centers, freestanding emergency rooms, micro-hospitals, and full-sized general acute care hospitals. While expanding AMD Global's healthcare footprint, Dr. Patel successfully created management and staffing companies for these facilities to provide back-office services, including accounting, supply-chain management, legal, HR, credentialing, and on-boarding support.

With over ten years of primary experience, Dr. Patel has created one of the most prominent revenue cycle management companies that specialize in both in-network and out-of-network professional and facility billing in the region.

"The key to a successful healthcare venture is the integration of development, lean operations, clinical processes, and an efficient, structured revenue cycle. Not many companies are able to marry all of these components into a cohesive business strategy, and this is why AMD Global stands out amongst the crowd of healthcare service companies. AMD not only develops, manages, invests and operates for others, but we also do the same for the facilities we wholly own."

Management services

Full-service healthcare operations

AMD manages every layer of healthcare operations — powered by technology, so facilities run efficiently, profitably, and at the highest standard of care.

Revenue cycle management

End-to-end billing optimization including CMS-1500 and CMS-1450 claims processing, Medicare and Medicaid reimbursement, and commercial payer contracting. Our dedicated billing team improves revenue cycle accuracy and reduces payment delays.

EHR / EMR platform

Tailored electronic health record platforms that improve treatment efficiency and elevate patients' continuity of care. Our systems eliminate redundant paperwork, automate administrative tasks, and facilitate prior authorizations seamlessly.

Business office management

Cross-silo management of medical business offices including administrative staffing, HR, scheduling, bookkeeping, budget management, and inter-department coordination — so clinical staff can focus on patient care.

Facility management

Comprehensive premises and equipment management including leasing, maintenance, repair, and long-term capital planning. AMD ensures every facility operates at peak performance and maintains its asset value over time.

Physician billing

Customizable CMS-1500 software suite that saves providers substantial time and money. Our system helps users quickly access patient information, expedite payments, and improve revenue cycle performance across Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers.

Operations management

Driven by a highly experienced management team overseeing operations across every AMD asset holding — providing data-driven policy structures, workforce engagement strategies, and health system financing to keep facilities running profitably.
