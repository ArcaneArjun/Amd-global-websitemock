Management services

Full-service healthcare operations

Beyond development, AMD manages every layer of healthcare operations — so facilities run efficiently, profitably, and at the highest standard of care.

Revenue cycle management

End-to-end billing optimization including CMS-1500 and CMS-1450 claims processing, Medicare/Medicaid reimbursement, and commercial payer contracting. Our dedicated billing team improves revenue cycle accuracy and reduces payment delays.

EHR / EMR platform

Tailored electronic health record and medical record platforms that improve treatment efficiency and elevate patients' continuity of care. Our systems eliminate redundant paperwork, automate administrative tasks, and facilitate prior authorizations seamlessly.

Business office management

Cross-silo management of medical business offices including administrative staffing, HR, scheduling, bookkeeping, budget management, and inter-department coordination. We streamline operations so clinical staff can focus on patient care.

Facility management

Comprehensive premises and equipment management including leasing, maintenance, repair, and long-term capital planning. AMD ensures every facility operates at peak performance and maintains its asset value over time.

Physician billing (1500)

Customizable CMS-1500 software suite that saves providers substantial time and money. Our system helps users quickly access patient information, expedite payments, and improve overall revenue cycle performance across Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers.

Operations management

Driven by a highly experienced management team overseeing operations across every AMD asset holding. We provide data-driven policy structures, workforce engagement strategies, and health system financing to keep facilities running profitably.
